Expert System Design for Duplexes

A fire protection project starts with careful planning. Our expert team at American Fire Protection Systems is skilled in the design of fire sprinkler systems for duplexes. We use state-of-the-art methods to make sure that each system is specifically designed to meet the unique needs of duplex properties while adhering to local codes and regulations.

Once we finalize the design, we submit it to the local Authority Having Jurisdiction (AHJ) for approval. This guarantees the system meets local ordinances, allowing us to proceed with installation confidently.

We understand that each duplex is different, so we approach each project with a personalized perspective. Our team meets your fire protection needs with proven designs that prioritize safety, compliance, and durability.

Precise Installation of Fire Sprinkler Systems

Reliable Maintenance and Inspections

A fire sprinkler system’s work doesn’t end after installation. For long-term protection, regular maintenance and inspections are essential. At American Fire Protection Systems, we offer reliable maintenance services to keep your fire sprinklers for duplexes in peak condition.
